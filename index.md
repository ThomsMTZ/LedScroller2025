### 1. Collection and Use of Personal Data

Pixel Scroller **does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable data** to external servers.

The application uses your device's local storage (via `AsyncStorage`) solely to save your user preferences, such as:

- The entered text
- The scrolling speed
- Selected colors and display options
- Recent history and favorite messages

These data remain strictly confined to your mobile device, and the Developer has no access to them.

### 2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

The application uses third-party services that may collect non-personal information (such as approximate IP address or
device type) for their basic functionality.

- **Google AdMob**:
  The app displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob. It is explicitly configured to serve only **non-personalized ads
  **. This means Google will not use your past behavior or advertising profile to target ads shown in Pixel Scroller.
  Ads are based purely on contextual information (such as your approximate city-level location or the content of the
  app).

  You can review Google's privacy policy here:
  [Google Policies & Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 3. Log Data

In case of an error in the app, data and information (through third-party products) on your phone, called **Log Data**,
may be collected. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device
name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing the Service, the time and date of your use
of the Service, and other technical statistics solely for bug-fixing purposes.

### 4. Security

Since the application does not collect or transmit any personal data over the Internet, the risk of your data being
breached through this Service is zero. The security of your local settings directly depends on the security of your own
mobile device (passcode lock, biometrics).

### 6. Children's Privacy
